Welcome Recruit

The Pennsylvania Army is a non-historically accurate European / North American community. We do private events on the weekend, ranging from line battles to normal skirmishes. We are a lax regiment in regards to attendance, but serious when it comes to gameplay.

Why should you join us? We aren’t special in regards to anyone else, same events, same days, but we have a unique and crazy playstyle, and we have an amazing community with an interesting mix of your average epic gamer, to military veterans and in-service members.

Come join the other Irish Brigade!

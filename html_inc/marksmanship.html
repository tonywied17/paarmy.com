Markmanship Test Rules

You may participate in the marksmanship test with one of four eligible rifles listed below. To obtain a gold medal or above you must have a bayonet equipped to your rifle.

Rifles:

  • Springfield M1861
  • Enfield M1853
  • Lorenz M1854
  • Mississippi 1841

Official Scoring & Medals

Target 1 (40 Yards)

  • Each soldier will get 7 shots on the 40 yard target.
  • For each outer-circle hit on the 40 yard target, you will be awarded 0 points,
  • for each inner-circle hit 2 points
  • for each bulls-eye you will get 5 points.

Target 2 (70 Yards)

  • Each soldier will get 6 shots on the 70 yard target.
  • For each hit on the outer-circle you will get 3 points
  • for each hit on the inner circle you will get 5 points
  • for each bulls-eye hit you will get 7 points.

Target 3 (125 Yards)

  • Each soldier will get 5 shots on the 125 yard targets.
  • For each hit on the board you are awarded 3 points
  • for each outer-circle hit is 5 points, each inner-circle hit is 7
  • For each bulls-eye hit is 10 points.

Medals

  • Platinum: 80 Points*
  • Gold: 60 Points*
  • Silver: 50 Points
  • Bronze: 30 Points
* - Bayonet Required
